Camps

Sanders Piano Studios offers a variety of fun-filled, educational, mini-music camps during the summer!

These intensive, mini-camps offer a great supplement to regular, weekly lessons or an alternative to summer lessons if you are going to be away for awhile. Students truly look forward to this change of pace each year as they dive into exploring their creative connection to music!

Composition camp:

Come go through the process of composing, performing and publishing a tune! Enter your composition into music notation software and create your own cover art. Learn to play your tune and perform for friends and family in a final celebration of your own unique music creation!

Duet camp:

Invite a friend to join you in your musical journey! Learn duets with a partner and work together every day to quickly acquire at least one duet you can play together. Explore duet piano literature and improve your sight-reading skills. Perform your duets for friends and family in a final celebration. 

Rhythm camp!

Come explore rhythm through body percussion, vocalisation, rhythm composition, percussion instruments and improvisation at the piano. Perform a rhythmic routine for your friends and family in a final celebration. 

Jazz/blues/pop camp:

Learn the basics of jazz improvisation at the piano through work on a tune from a leadsheet. Learn some simple jazz harmony and comping techniques and learn how to walk a bass line. In this camp you will acquire some basic techniques for improvising, make listening a daily habit and participate in a final performance with a rhythm section (piano, drums and bass!).
